Cultural Insights

Must-Try Foods

Indulge in biryani, nihari, seekh kebabs, and traditional sweets like gulab jamun and jalebi.

Festivals & Traditions

Experience vibrant celebrations like Eid, Basant, Shandur Polo Festival, and Lok Mela.

Local Hospitality

Pakistanis are known for their warm hospitality. Experience traditional "mehman nawazi" (guest treatment).

Travel Tips

Transportation

Use ride-hailing apps in cities. For intercity travel, consider trains or reputable bus services.

Accommodations

Book hotels in advance, especially during peak season. Consider guesthouses for authentic experiences.

Safety

Pakistan is generally safe for tourists. Stay aware of your surroundings and follow local advice.

Communication

English is widely understood in urban areas. Learn basic Urdu phrases for rural regions.
